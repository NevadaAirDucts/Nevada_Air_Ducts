How Often To Replace An Air Filter?




HVAC air filters vary in size and function depending on the brand of the filter and your heating and cooling system. Generally,
your air filter should be replaced at least once every 90-days. Most homes and offices typically have their HVAC running
all day to prevent the extreme temperatures of Las Vegas from affecting them. Because of this, the air filter can get very dirty
quickly. Las Vegas is known for its desert landscaping, so dust, dirt, and other allergens are no stranger to the city. These allergens can make
their way into your air duct system, which can cause your allergies to flare up and cause poor air quality. However, routine
air filter replacement is necessary to minimize exposure.

Benefits of Residential Chimney Cleaning

Here are the benefits of residential chimney cleaning:

  • Decreases fire hazards
  • Improves home safety
  • Increases chimney functionality
  • Extends chimney lifespan


The biggest benefit of cleaning your chimney is that it decreases any fire hazards from a blocked chimney. Woodfires produce soot and other contaminants that get filtered out from your home through the chimney. Over time, these particles can clog up your chimney and block the direction of the smoke. This is extremely dangerous since soot is extremely flammable, and the direction of the smoke can stay within your home, which increases carbon monoxide levels. AirDuct Cleaning recommends a chimney cleaning at least once a year. This keeps your home safe, clean, and protected from any issues that may come from your chimney.
